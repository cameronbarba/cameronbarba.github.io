Hydr-8

January 2019

A concept app that tracks the amount of water a user consumes throughout the day. Attempts to create an engaging and educational interface that can serve as the only interaction point between the consumer and product.

Minimizes friction and noise by providing only essential information to the user.

Problem:

Often I think I'm drinking enough water throughout the day. However, I quickly noticed that my estimates were extremely optimistic.

Solution:

I wanted to create a visually appealing interface that would provide instant feedback on my current water consumption. I performed an audit of competitors and boiled down potential user needs to their most simple forms.

Role:
Designer

Duration:
2 days

Client:
Personal Project

Skills Involved:
UX, Photoshop, Keynote, Sketch
